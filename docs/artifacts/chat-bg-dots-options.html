The empty-state of the full-page chat is prime real estate for a little character. Here are six treatments — geometric, atmospheric, textural — all kept whisper-subtle (it's still a bank tool) and shown on the app's real palette in both themes. My take

Dots are the safe standard — genuinely hard to get wrong. But since you want interesting, there's an obvious ownable move: the brick lattice (Option 2) quietly echoes the Bricklayer emblem — personality without noise. If you'd rather have mood than geometry, Aurora (3). Any of these can pair with the composer-fade idea from before, and all respect reduced-motion (they're static).
